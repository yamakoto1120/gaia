Weddings

We’re there to capture the joy, laughter, emotion and beauty of your special day.

Our aim is to create beautiful, natural photographs that capture the spirit of your day – from the biggest moment to the smallest detail. We’re happy to customise something to suit your needs and there are many extras available including a variety of professional wedding photography albums, engagement shoots, photo booth and more

There are many terms used to describe our style of natural wedding photography, so it can be a little confusing when researching into the different types and styles of wedding photographers for your day.

Our discreet approach to a more authentic and honest style as a wedding photographer is often created using natural and available light only and is most commonly described as ‘reportage wedding photography’ or ‘documentary wedding photography’.

In essence, these terms describe the same fundamental, candid style of wedding photography – one that doesn’t dictate or direct but simply observes and discreetly captures genuine, un-staged and spontaneous moments as they unfold, naturally. To see what this means in practice, look through the selection of wedding photographs below where you will see the kind of genuine and truthful style of wedding images you will receive from us.

Other than the odd, pre-planned traditional family group photo, We typically spend 95% of the wedding day photographing real and meaningful moments between family and friends. Weddings are full of these beautiful and often fleeting moments – elements that depict the true essence of a wedding day.

A skilled natural-style wedding photographer is one that you shouldn’t notice too much and yet has the competence to spend the entire day in the thick of the action photographing uninhibited interactions between family and friends. Importantly, a photographer should have no impact on the atmosphere or flow of the day itself.



Events

Whether your planning a party, celebration or event, our relaxed documentary photography will capture all the special moments.

We’re there to allow you to have fun while we capture relaxed, genuine photos that you can cherish forever.
Whether you’re planning a:

- Big Birthday Party
- School Prom
- Family Celebration
- Anniversary Party
- Engagement Party
- Office Party
- Summer Event

we have your event covered.
Our Event Photography comes in two great options;

Roaming Party Photography

We capture all the little details of your event before getting great, natural, pictures of your family and guests as they party the night away. With no studio or set base your photographer is free to roam wherever the action takes them and produce wonderful images for you to treasure.

Mini Photo Studio

We arrive before your guests and set-up a mini size professional studio. We’ll provide studio style lights and use either;
- A sparkly background
- A white contemporary background
- An attractive architectural feature at your venue.

If your celebration is formal we’ll take wonderful portraits. If your event is fun we can bring a range of props and create an open photobooth. Or, we can combine both elements – the choice is yours.

Using a temporary photo studio we take glamorous, fun photos of your guests dressed in their formal attire. Following their picture session guests can receive a high quality photo print. Prints are produced instantly for guests to take as a memento of the event.

Our temporary photo studios include a range of backgrounds that include;

- Formal Black
- Contemporary White.
- Photo background options to suite your theme
We are also happy to use a feature within your venue such a showpiece fireplace or a stunning staircase.

When we’re asked to provide on-site printing for events we produce prints that are instantly dry to the touch. The printed pictures are presented in an attractive photo mount which we then place in a protective bag.

Guests can either purchase their prints on the night for a typical price of £10.00 each or the event organiser can pre-purchase an agreed number of prints at a reduced cost paid in advance of the event. Following the event we set-up a secure on-line gallery where guests can purchase re-prints or additional copies of their pictures.

Instant on-site printing at Charity Events

Using an Event Photographer that provides on-site printing is a proven way for charity events and charity balls to raise additional donations. We will happily donate up-to 10% of the print sales achieved on the night to the charity via the organisers.

Depending on the layout of your event it may be possible for the event photographer to also take pictures of your room setting and some roaming pictures during the entertainment. Just let us know if you’d like this included when we discuss your event.

So, if you are looking for professional and friendly event photography that provides guests with high quality photos and on-site printing contact us now to discuss your forthcoming event.

Wedding & Event Albums

We design and create a range of premium, professional wedding and event albums for my photography packages but also other wedding photographers, artists and couples who are in need of a high end, custom wedding photo album service in the UK.

There’s an incredible feeling when you’re presented with a photo album of wedding or event images to look through, photographs are often studied for a little longer and the quality of the photographic printing can really be appreciated. Looking at digital wedding albums online or screen, no matter how large the images, just isn’t the same.

We do worry that many of the family portrait and wedding photographs that we and other photographers produce will just sit on a hard drive or a smartphone and be forgotten. It’s the very opposite of an album. A beautifully printed, large wedding album is simply the best way to look over the photos and relive the day. This is especially so with documentary-style photography as it allows you to ponder just that little bit longer over narrative and context.

All our handmade wedding photo albums are carefully designed to showcase your story to its full potential across the wedding day – custom photo albums are highly personalised to the customers need, often with couples playing a significant role in the design stages. You can essentially make your own professional wedding album, showcasing your photos, to an exceptionally high standard, tailored to your unique taste. All our photo albums are handmade using only the very best leather, papers and printing materials available.

Already a customer? If you’re interested in adding an album to display your reportage wedding or portrait photography, please complete the contact form or email us directly with your style preferences and requirements. We regularly create wedding anniversary albums, and the fact that the first year is traditionally celebrated with paper is very fitting!

The first design proof is ready within 3 weeks and there are unlimited tweaks to this design, once an event or wedding album design is approved orders are produced and delivered within a further 2 to 3 weeks.
