The aim of this is to map what we find to be benefits in all the concepts.
The benefits for positive concepts are active, meaning that someone would actively want to engage with them by emotionally enjoying them to the maximum, while the benefits of negative concepts are passive, meaning that they are used to provide a clear and rational perspective on the way in which they function.
To put it another way, the things which are generally accepted as good, are the things we enjoy. Meanwhile, those which are generally accepted as bad, are the things we need to look at rationally.
The way in which this is done, is by not only using but being one with the infinite powerful foundation of logic itself.
